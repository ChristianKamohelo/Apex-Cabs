Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our premium cab services and transportation solutions.

You can book a cab in multiple ways: through our website, mobile app, or by calling our 24/7 hotline at (+266)59400531 APEX-CABS. Simply provide your pickup location, destination, and preferred time. We accept both advance bookings and on-demand requests with typical arrival times of 10-30 minutes.

Our fleet includes luxury sedans, SUVs, and premium eco-friendly vehicles. Choose from our Apex Phantom GT for executive travel, Apex Sovereign EV for eco-friendly rides, or Apex Velocity R for special occasions. All vehicles feature leather interiors, climate control, and premium amenities for maximum comfort.

We operate 24/7 throughout Maseru CBD, and surrounding metropolitan areas. Our service extends up to 50 miles from city centers, covering airports, hotels, business districts, and residential areas. Weekend and holiday service is available with private Chauffeur.

Our transparent pricing is based on distance, time, and vehicle type. No surge pricing or hidden fees. You'll receive an upfront quote before booking, and we accept all major credit cards, mobile payments, and corporate accounts. Group discounts and membership rates are also available.

All our drivers undergo extensive background checks and professional training. Vehicles are equipped with GPS tracking, emergency communication systems, and are sanitized between rides. We maintain comprehensive insurance coverage and provide real-time ride tracking for passenger safety and peace of mind.
